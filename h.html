FlipKart:The Shopping Destination

Flipkart , a Indian e-commerce website was founded by two persons namely Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal in the year 2007. Sachin and Binny were alumini of Indian Institute of technology Delhi and former amazon employees. Tired of working at the office , both of them came together and thought of developing their own startup company .With other e-commerce websites already existing that time , "Fabmart.com" being the first indian website founded by 'K. Vaitheeswaran' were not developed properly . They did not have proper design nor they were able to build trust of the customers.
This gave Sachin and Binny Bansal an idea of developing a platform with the best design and features. Thus in 2007 flipkart , an e-commerce platform was finally developed. Initailly they focused on online book sale with country-wide shipping . By 2008 flipkart started receiving hundred orders per day and was growing slowly .Today flipkart is the biggest Indian online e- marketplace competing with the world leader amazon.
